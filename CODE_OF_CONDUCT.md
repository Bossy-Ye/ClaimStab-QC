# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a collaborative, respectful, and inclusive community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus critiques on ideas and implementation details, not people.
- Share context and evidence when disagreeing.
- Help newcomers onboard.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or repeated hostile interactions.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Scope
This code of conduct applies to project spaces, issues, pull requests, and related community discussions.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private report to the maintainers.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, moderate discussions, or restrict participation for violations.
